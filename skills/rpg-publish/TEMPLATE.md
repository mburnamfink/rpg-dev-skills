## GM Briefing

*The whole truth, in tight beats — the ONE place the reasoning is explained. Everything the Player
Pitch withholds: the real situation, why the central mechanics work as they do, the reveal order,
how it ends. A GM reads this once in prep and never again. Keep it beats, not essay.*

## GM Summary

*One page to run from at a glance. Dense and scannable.*
- **Clocks:** each named clock — size · what fills it · what it triggers.
- **Cast:** one line each — who they are and the single lever they offer. Link: [Name](#anchor).
- **The shape:** fixed beats vs. floating scenes; the master clock; the sequence/map.
- **Remember:** the 3–6 easy-to-drop facts that break the session if forgotten.
